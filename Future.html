Future Air Defense Programs

Akash-NG

Next-generation SAM with 70 km range and indigenous RF seeker.

Project Kusha

Indigenous long-range SAM (~350 km) augmenting S-400.

BMD Phase-2

AD-1/AD-2 interceptors targeting missiles up to 5,000 km.

QRSAM & VSHORAD

Quick-reaction and very short-range systems for mobile protection.

AI Integration

Autonomous threat assessment and decision-making in systems like Indrajaal.

Key Challenges

Budget Constraints

High costs of advanced systems and R&D.

Technological Gaps

Hypersonic and stealth interception require cutting-edge research.

Geopolitical Pressures

Sanctions risk on foreign procurements (e.g., CAATSA sanctions on S-400).

Evolving Threats

Rapid adaptation needed for drone swarms, cyber attacks, and space-based threats.
