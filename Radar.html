Radar Infrastructure

Rohini 3d Radar

  • Developed by DRDO, produced by BEL; operates in S-band.

  • Detects targets beyond 200 km and tracks 150–200 simultaneously.

  • Determines target altitude via passive phased-array beams.

  • Mounted on mobile platforms for flexibility.

  • Provides early warning for Akash missile batteries.

Rajendra Fire-Control Radar

  • C-band phased-array radar linked to Akash launchers.

  • Tracks up to 64 targets within a 150 km range.

  • Guides missiles using a secure command link.

  • Equipped with ECCM to resist jamming.

Swordfish LRTR

  • L-band AESA radar for ballistic missile defense.

  • Based on Israel's Green Pine technology.

  • Tracks targets up to 800 km away.

  • Detects small objects like reentry vehicles with precision.

Green Pine / Arudhra

  • Green Pine imported between 2002–2005 for BMD.

  • Arudhra is an indigenous mid-range radar developed by DRDO.

  • Both used for missile defense and early warning systems.

AWACS & Netra AEW&C

  • Phalcon AWACS on IL-76: 360° airborne surveillance.

  • Netra AEW&C on Embraer: detects low-flying threats.

  • Integrated into IACCS to form a Recognized Air Situation Picture (RASP).

  • Enables real-time threat tracking and automated defense responses.
